Recipe #1

(Did not find a better picture :D)

  • Time: 35 minutes
  • Serves: 5
  • Difficulty: 2/10
Ingredients Quantity
Pasta, water, minced meat, tomato paste, garlic, oil 500g, 3l, 300g, 150g, 3p

Step 1: Heat the oil in a frying pan.

Step 2: Put minced meat in the pan.

Step 3: Fry for 5 minutes on each side.

Step 4: Pour the water, lower the heat and leave the pan with closed lid.

Step 5: Cook pasta in another pot for 7 minutes.

Step 6: Add tomato paste and garlic into the pan with meat.

Step 7: Mix pasta with meat after 10 minutes


Additional information

Except these steps, you can also supplement the recipe with various herbs to your taste.
Don't be afraid to mix different ingredients and spices.
After all, we cook for fun!

Recipe #2

  • Time: 50 minutes
  • Serves: 4
  • Difficulty: 4/10
Ingredients Quantity
Flour, dough, eggs, butter, sgustchenka, sugar, milk 1 cup, 400g, 1p, 120g, 100g, 1 tablespoon, 120ml

Step 1: In a bowl add flour, sugar and gently mix.

Step 2: Add butter and combine until mixture is crumbly, now add milk and mix, wrap and chill for 30 minutes

Step 3: Dust the surface with flour and roll the dough in rectantular shape.

Step 4: Cut the dough into triangle. Place sgustchenka on wider edge of triangle.

Step 5: Preheat the overn to 200 degrees brush the egg wash, bake for 8 minutes.

Step 6: Reduce the temperature to 180 degrees and bake for 12 to 16 minutes.


Additional information

The ideal conditon would be a golden crust on a dough, to obtain it follow all the steps above.
Croissant could be considered as a breakfast or dessert, the very best match is a black coffe without sugar.

Recipe #3

  • Time: 35 minutes
  • Serves: 4
  • Difficulty: 4/10
Ingredients Quantity
Flour, mushrooms, carrots, garlic, water, salt 2 cups, 6p, 2p, 5-6p, 2cups, 1.5 tablespoons

Step 1: Knead a dough of flour using water. Now cover the kneaded dough with a cling cloth and leave it for 10-15 minutes.

Step 2: Meanwhile, mix all the remaining ingredients, mushrooms, salt, carrots and garlic in a bowl. Keep the stuffing aside.

Step 3: Knead the dough again and then break them into small round portions. Roll each portion into a small ball.

Step 4: Keep the middle of the rolled ball thick to create a base and prevent it from breaking. Keep the edges of the ball much thinner.

Step 5: Put the stuffing in the middle of the rolled ball. Wet your fingers and close the ball completely.

Step 6: Repeat the same process with the remaining dough and stuffing. Place the momos into a large steamer and over the boiling water. If you want, you can grease the steamer dish with a little oil to prevent the dumplings from sticking.

Step 7: Make sure the momos are placed at a little distance and do not stick to each other. Steam the momos for 8-10 minutes on a low flame.


Additional information

The best way to eat these dumplings is to consume them right after boiling with adding some souce on your taste. Feel free to experiment with amount and kinds of ingredients you put into dough.
Originally this meal comes from ancient China.
